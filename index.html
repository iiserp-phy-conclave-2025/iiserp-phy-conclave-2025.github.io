  • Physics Department at IISER, Pune

The Physics Conclave at IISER Pune is designed to foster meaningful dialogue within the physics community, highlight cutting-edge research, and inspire collaborative efforts. The theme of the event for 2025 is "Contemporary Questions in Physics: The Important & The Interesting." Below are some of the key highlights:

  • Panel Discussions: Faculty members from diverse sub-disciplines will engage in discussions around critical research questions, followed by an open, interactive session with the audience.
  • Poster Presentations: Physics research students will present their latest work in dedicated poster sessions, offering a platform for sharing new ideas and innovations.
